Welcome to Glebe Heights

We are a close-knit residential community nestled along the shores of the Chesapeake Bay in Edgewater, Maryland.

The Glebe Heights Community Association brings together neighbors who share a love for waterfront living. Our community enjoys a private beach, dock, and gathering spaces where families connect, kids play, and lifelong friendships are forged.

Whether you're new to the neighborhood or a longtime resident, there's always something happening in Glebe Heights. From holiday celebrations to summer cookouts at the fire pit, our community is what we make it together.

Beach Area

Our sandy community beach is the heart of Glebe Heights. Perfect for swimming, sunbathing, or just watching the sunset over the water. Beach chairs are available for all residents.

Our shared dock provides water access for fishing, kayaking, paddle boarding, and enjoying the Chesapeake Bay right from our neighborhood.

Beach Reservations

Reserve our community beach area for your private event

How to Reserve

Glebe Heights residents can reserve the community beach area for private gatherings such as birthday parties, family reunions, or special celebrations.

  • Available to all Glebe Heights residents in good standing
  • Reservations include access to the beach, fire pit, chairs, and dock area
  • Please submit requests at least 2 weeks in advance
  • Reservations are first-come, first-served
  • The beach remains open to all residents even during reservations unless otherwise noted
Request a Reservation

Guidelines

  • Clean up after your event — leave it better than you found it
  • Respect quiet hours after 10 PM
  • No glass containers on the beach
  • Supervise children at all times near the water
  • Extinguish the fire pit completely when finished
  • Report any damage or maintenance needs to the association
