Membership Information

Ownership or residence in the Glebe Heights community makes you eligible to participate in the Glebe Heights Community Association. However, membership in the association is required for access to the beach and pier facilities.

Your annual dues support the maintenance and improvement of our shared waterfront spaces, community events, and neighborhood infrastructure.

Terms of Use Agreement

Glebe Heights Community Association — Terms of Use

ARTICLE X — Use of Facilities

  1. Non-members shall be denied the use of all community association facilities.
  2. One ID card shall be issued per membership. Guests must be accompanied by a member at all times.
  3. One gate key shall be issued per membership (Beach level). One boat ramp key shall be issued per membership (Beach + Boat Ramp level). Keys may not be duplicated or shared.

ARTICLE XV — Beach & Property Rules

  1. The beach area is for members and their accompanied guests only.
  2. The boat ramp is for use by paid boat ramp members only.
  3. Two (2) parking passes shall be issued per household.
  4. Fires are permitted only in the designated fire pit and must be attended at all times.
  5. No persons shall be on the beach after 11:00 PM.
  6. Members are responsible for keeping the beach and facilities clean. Please clean up after yourself and your guests.
  7. No animals are permitted on the beach. Animals are allowed on other community property provided owners clean up after them.
  8. No trailer storage on community property for more than 24 hours.
  9. Parking in the community lot is for members only.

By signing below, I acknowledge that I have read, understand, and agree to abide by the Glebe Heights Community Association Terms of Use.
